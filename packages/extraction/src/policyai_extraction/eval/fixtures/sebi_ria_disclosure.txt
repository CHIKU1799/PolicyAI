Securities and Exchange Board of India
Circular No. SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD/P/CIR/2026/112

Sub: Enhanced disclosure and record-keeping norms for Investment Advisers

This circular is issued in exercise of powers under Section 11(1) of the Securities
and Exchange Board of India Act, 1992 read with the SEBI (Investment Advisers)
Regulations, 2013. It is applicable to all persons registered as Investment Advisers
(IAs) and shall come into force with effect from 1 October 2026.

1. Suitability and risk profiling
1.1 Every Investment Adviser shall carry out risk profiling of the client and
maintain documented evidence of suitability assessment before recommending any
product.

2. Fee disclosure
2.1 The Investment Adviser shall disclose the fee structure, including the mode of
fee and any conflict of interest, in writing to the client at the time of onboarding.
2.2 The adviser shall not receive any consideration by way of commission for the
products recommended.

3. Record-keeping
3.1 The Investment Adviser shall maintain records of interactions and advice
provided to clients for a period of five years.
3.2 Records shall be made available to SEBI on request.

4. Annual audit
4.1 Every Investment Adviser shall conduct an annual audit of compliance with the
IA Regulations and submit the audit report to SEBI within six months of the end of
the financial year.

Failure to comply with this circular shall be treated as a violation of the IA
Regulations and may attract enforcement action including suspension of registration.
