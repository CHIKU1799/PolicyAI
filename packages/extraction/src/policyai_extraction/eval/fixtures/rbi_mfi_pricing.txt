Master Direction — Regulation of Microfinance Loans (Pricing and Conduct)

This Master Direction is issued under Sections 45L and 45M of the Reserve Bank of
India Act, 1934 and is applicable to all Non-Banking Financial Company –
Microfinance Institutions (NBFC-MFIs) and other NBFCs undertaking microfinance
business. It comes into effect from 1 August 2026.

1. Pricing of microfinance loans
1.1 Each regulated entity shall put in place a board-approved policy on the pricing
of microfinance loans, covering the interest rate, processing fee and any other
charge. The pricing policy shall be reviewed at least annually by the board.
1.2 There shall be no pre-payment penalty on microfinance loans.

2. Disclosure and transparency
2.1 The regulated entity shall disclose the effective annualised interest rate to
the borrower in the Key Fact Statement before sanction.
2.2 A standardised fact sheet shall be displayed prominently in every branch and on
the entity's website.

3. Conduct and grievance redressal
3.1 The entity shall constitute a grievance redressal mechanism and designate a
nodal officer. The contact details of the nodal officer shall be displayed at all
offices.
3.2 Recovery shall be carried out only at a designated place and recovery agents
shall be trained in accordance with the Fair Practices Code.

4. Reporting
4.1 Regulated entities shall submit a quarterly return on microfinance pricing to
the Reserve Bank within 15 days of the end of each quarter.

5. Supersession
This Master Direction supersedes the circular on Pricing of Credit for Microfinance
issued earlier.

Non-compliance with this Direction may attract penalty under the provisions of the
Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934, including monetary penalty and supervisory action.
